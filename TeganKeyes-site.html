Geometry

Geometric Formulas for area A, circumfrence C, and volume V

Triangle

  1. A = (1/2)(bh)

Circle

  1. A = (pi)r^2
  2. C = 2(pi)r

Sector of a Circle

  1. A = (1/2)(r^2)(theta)
  2. s = (r)(theta) (theta in radians)

Sphere

  1. V = (4/3)(pi)(r^3)
  2. A = 4(pi)(r^2)

Cylinder

  1. V = (pi)(r^2)(h)

Cone

  1. V = (1/3)(pi)(r^2)(h)
  2. A = (pi)(r)sqrt((r^2)+(h^2))
