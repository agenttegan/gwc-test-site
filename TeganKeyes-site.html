Geometric Formulas for area A, circumfrence C, and volume V

Triangle

Circle

Sector of a Circle

  1. A = (½)(r2)(θ)

Sphere

  1. V = (4/3)π(r3)

Cylinder

  1. V = π(r2)(h)

Cone
