Geometric Formulas for area A, circumfrence C, and volume V

Triangle

  1. A = ½bh

Circle

  1. A = πr^2
  2. C = 2πr

Sector of a Circle

  1. A = (½)(r^2)(θ)
  2. s = rθ (θ in radians)

Sphere

  1. V = (4/3)π(r^3)
  2. A = 4(π)(r^2)

Cylinder

  1. V = π(r^2)(h)

Cone

  1. V = (1/3)π(r^2)(h)
  2. A = πrsqrt((r^2)+(h^2))
